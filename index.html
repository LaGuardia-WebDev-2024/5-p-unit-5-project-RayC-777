What Our Show Is About:

West Side Story is a modern retelling of Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet, set in the 1950s in New York City. The story revolves around two rival gangs, the Jets and the Sharks, and the forbidden love between Tony, a former Jet, and Maria, the sister of the Sharks' leader. As tensions rise between the gangs, Tony and Maria's love faces numerous challenges, leading to a tragic conclusion. The musical explores themes of love, racial prejudice, and the pursuit of the American Dream, all set against a backdrop of memorable music and dance. Ionic numbers such as "Tonight," "America," and "Somewhere" have become timeless classics, making West Side Story a beloved piece of American musical theater, and a thrill to perform, and share the story with audiences.
